## Marketing Channels

### Tier 1: High Priority (Do First)

#### 1. Warm Network Outreach
**Owner:** All three partners
**Cost:** $0
**Timeline:** Ongoing from Day 1

**Action:**
- Each partner lists 20+ contacts who might:
  - Work in HR/People roles
  - Own or run a business
  - Know someone who does

- Ask for introductions, not sales

**Script:**
> "Hey [Name], I've just launched something new with two colleagues—immersive team experiences using voice, movement, and music. We're looking for a few pilot clients. Do you know anyone in an HR or people role who might be interested in hearing about it?"

**Target:** 60 conversations in first 2 months → 3-5 warm leads → 1-2 pilots

## Sales Process

### Lead → Client Journey

```
Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Decision → Delivery → Advocacy
```

### Touchpoints

1. **Awareness**
   - LinkedIn post / content
   - Referral mention
   - Website visit

2. **Interest**
   - Website deep dive
   - Download/read one-pager
   - LinkedIn connection

3. **Consideration**
   - Discovery call (30 mins)
   - Proposal sent
   - References provided

4. **Decision**
   - Follow-up call
   - Contract signed
   - Deposit paid

5. **Delivery**
   - Consultation
   - Session
   - Follow-up summary

6. **Advocacy**
   - Testimonial request
   - Referral request
   - Stay in touch (newsletter)

### Conversion Targets

| Stage | Target Conversion |
|-------|-------------------|
| Enquiry → Discovery call | 80% |
| Discovery call → Proposal | 70% |
| Proposal → Booking | 50% |
| **Enquiry → Booking** | **28%** |

To get 8 bookings/month (Year 2 target):
- Need ~29 enquiries/month
- Need ~23 discovery calls/month

## Appendix: Templates

### Pitch Email Template

**Subject:** Something different for [Company]'s next team day?

Hi [Name],

[Mutual connection] mentioned you might be thinking about team development for [Company].

We've just launched Momentum—immersive team experiences using voice, movement, and live music. It's not your typical team-building. We help teams connect in ways that talking alone can't achieve.

Quick context: Our team includes Darren Percival (The Voice Australia), Linda Lou Küster (psychology + movement therapy), and myself (20 years teaching at Griffith Conservatorium).

Would you be open to a 20-minute call to see if it might be a fit? Happy to share what we're hearing from similar teams.

No pressure either way—just thought it might be relevant.

Best,
Dave

---

### Discovery Call Agenda (30 mins)

1. **(5 min)** Intro + rapport
2. **(10 min)** Learn about their team
   - What's prompting this?
   - What have they tried before?
   - What does success look like?
3. **(10 min)** Share what we do
   - Brief overview
   - Relevant example/story
   - How we'd approach their situation
4. **(5 min)** Next steps
   - Answer questions
   - Propose sending a proposal
   - Agree timeline
