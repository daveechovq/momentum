## Break-Even Analysis

### Monthly Fixed Costs
- Insurance: $100
- Website/software: $70
- Accounting: $125
- Marketing: $300
- Materials: $100
- Travel buffer: $150
- **Total Monthly Fixed:** ~$850

### Break-Even Sessions
$850 ÷ $6,000 = **0.14 sessions/month**

**Insight:** You're profitable from your first session. The business has very low fixed costs.

## Tax Considerations

*Note: Consult with accountant for specific advice*

### GST
- Required if turnover exceeds $75,000 (highly likely)
- Register for GST from start
- All prices quoted + GST
- Quarterly BAS lodgement

### Partnership Tax
- Partnership doesn't pay tax directly
- Each partner reports their share on personal return
- Quarterly PAYG instalments likely required
- Set aside ~30% of distributions for tax

### Deductions (Likely)
- Home office (portion used for Momentum)
- Phone/internet (business portion)
- Vehicle (travel to sessions)
- Equipment
- Professional development
