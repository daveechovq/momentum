## 3. Profit & Loss Sharing

### 3.1 Standard Split
Profits and losses to be shared equally: **33.33% each**

### 3.2 Distribution Schedule
- Monthly distribution of profits after:
  - Operating expenses paid
  - Reserve maintained (minimum $5,000)
- Distributions within 14 days of month end

### 3.3 Tax
Each partner responsible for their own tax obligations. Partnership to provide annual income statements.

## 5. Decision Making

### 5.1 Day-to-Day Decisions
- Any partner may make operational decisions under $500
- Dave handles booking/scheduling unless conflict arises

### 5.2 Significant Decisions (Require Majority)
- Expenses over $500
- New service offerings
- Pricing changes
- Marketing campaigns over $1,000

### 5.3 Major Decisions (Require Unanimous)
- Admitting new partners
- Taking on debt
- Changing profit split
- Selling the business
- Changing business structure
- Capital calls over $2,000

### 5.4 Dispute Resolution
1. Discussion between partners
2. If unresolved: Mediation (agreed mediator)
3. If still unresolved: Arbitration (binding)

### 7.1 Bank Account

- Transactions under $2,000: Any single signatory

## 8. Non-Compete & Confidentiality

### 8.1 During Partnership
Partners agree not to:
- Provide competing team facilitation services independently
- Solicit Momentum clients for personal services
- Share confidential client information

**Exceptions:**
- Darren: Existing vocal coaching and performance work
- Linda: Existing ZenThai therapy clients
- Dave: Existing teaching and performance work

### 8.2 After Departure
- 12-month non-compete for substantially similar services in Brisbane metro
- Ongoing confidentiality for client information
- No solicitation of Momentum clients for 12 months

## 9. Intellectual Property

### 9.1 Existing IP
Each partner retains ownership of IP they bring to the partnership:
- Darren: "The Singing Space" methodology, existing content
- Linda: ZenThai training, existing content
- Dave: Musical compositions, existing content

### 9.2 Partnership IP
IP created specifically for Momentum:
- Jointly owned by all partners equally
- Includes: Session designs, branded materials, client resources

### 9.3 Brand
"Momentum" brand and associated materials owned by partnership.

---

## 10. Withdrawal of a Partner

### 10.1 Voluntary Withdrawal
- 90 days written notice required
- Departing partner entitled to:
  - Share of profits to departure date
  - Return of capital contribution (less any losses)
  - Buyout of partnership share (see 10.3)

### 10.2 Involuntary Withdrawal
A partner may be required to withdraw by unanimous vote of other partners if:
- Material breach of this agreement
- Conduct harmful to the business
- Incapacity exceeding 6 months
- Bankruptcy or insolvency

### 10.3 Buyout Valuation
- Value determined by: 1x annual revenue (trailing 12 months) × partner percentage
- Payment: 50% within 90 days, 50% within 12 months
- Alternative: Independent valuation if disputed

### 10.4 Continuation
Remaining partners may continue the business or wind up by mutual agreement.

---

## 11. Death or Incapacity

### 11.1 Temporary Incapacity
- Partner's share continues for up to 6 months
- Other partners cover responsibilities
- Adjusted profit share if extended

### 11.2 Permanent Incapacity or Death
- Partnership share transfers to estate/nominee
- Remaining partners have right to buy out within 12 months
- Valuation per section 10.3

---

## 12. Dissolution

### 12.1 Triggers
Partnership dissolves upon:
- Unanimous agreement to dissolve
- Withdrawal of a partner (unless others continue)
- Court order
- Bankruptcy of partnership

### 12.2 Winding Up
1. Complete existing client commitments
2. Collect receivables
3. Pay debts and obligations
4. Distribute remaining assets per capital accounts
5. Divide jointly-owned IP (or sell and divide proceeds)

---
