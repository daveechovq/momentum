## Executive Summary

Momentum delivers transformational team experiences through voice, movement, and music. We help organisations build genuine human connection—improving collaboration, communication, and wellbeing.

**The Opportunity:** Traditional team-building is stale and ineffective. Organisations are spending money on activities that don't create lasting change. Momentum offers something different: evidence-based, immersive experiences that transform how teams connect.

**The Team:** Three complementary specialists with 60+ years combined experience in vocal performance, movement therapy, and music education.

**The Goal:** 2 sessions per week at $6,000 average, generating $576,000 annual revenue within 18 months.

## Operations

### Delivery Model

Each session requires all three facilitators:
- **Darren:** Leads vocal expression, group dynamics
- **Linda:** Leads movement, grounding, bodywork
- **Dave:** Provides live music, supports facilitation, operations

### Capacity

| Per Week | Sessions | Revenue |
|----------|----------|---------|
| Minimum | 1 | $6,000 |
| Target | 2 | $12,000 |
| Maximum | 3 | $18,000 |

Maximum 3 sessions/week to maintain quality and prevent burnout.

### Equipment & Materials
- Portable PA system (Darren has)
- Yoga mats (10) — $300
- Portable keyboard — Dave has
- Printed materials — $50/session
- Miscellaneous props — $200

### Operational Workflow

1. **Enquiry** → Respond within 24 hours
2. **Discovery Call** → 30 mins, assess fit
3. **Proposal** → Within 48 hours
4. **Booking** → 50% deposit, signed agreement
5. **Consultation** → 1-2 weeks before, detailed intake
6. **Delivery** → Session day
7. **Follow-up** → Summary document within 1 week
8. **Feedback** → Request testimonial

## Risk Analysis

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Slow initial uptake | Medium | High | Pilot sessions at reduced rate, leverage networks |
| Key person unavailable | Medium | High | Cross-train basic elements, have backup plan |
| Economic downturn | Low | High | Diversify to schools/sports (less budget-sensitive) |
| Competitor copies model | Low | Medium | Build reputation, relationships, continuous innovation |
| Negative client experience | Low | High | Thorough intake, manage expectations, feedback loops |
