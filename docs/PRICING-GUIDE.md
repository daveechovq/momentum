## Pricing Psychology

### Why This Pricing Works

**$4,500 Half-Day:**
- Per-person cost (~$225) comparable to premium training
- Below threshold for most L&D budgets requiring multiple approvals
- Room for negotiation without going below $4,000

**$6,500 Full-Day:**
- Natural "upgrade" from half-day
- Better value per hour ($928/hr vs $1,125/hr)
- Covers full day of facilitator time + prep

### Anchoring Strategy

Always quote Full-Day first, then Half-Day as alternative:
> "A Full-Day Immersion would give your team the deepest experience at $6,500. If timing is tight, we also offer a Half-Day at $4,500."

## Negotiation Guidelines

### Flexible On:
- Payment terms (can do 50/50 or staged)
- Timing (early morning, evening)
- Program structure (within reason)
- Add-on bundling

### Firm On:
- Minimum price (don't go below $4,000 half-day / $5,500 full-day)
- All three facilitators present
- Adequate prep time (minimum 1 week notice)
- Professional terms (contract required)

### Red Flags (Decline or Caution):
- Wants to "try before they buy" with free session
- Comparing to $500 team-building activities
- Unclear on objectives, unwilling to consult
- Disrespectful of time or expertise
