# Momentum Launch Checklist

## Phase 1: Foundation (Do First)

### Business Structure
- [ ] Decide on business structure (partnership agreement between Darren, Linda, Dave)
- [ ] Register business name (check availability: ABN lookup, ASIC)
- [ ] Apply for ABN
- [ ] Open business bank account
- [ ] Set up accounting (Xero, MYOB, or similar)
- [ ] Determine profit-sharing arrangement
- [ ] Draft simple partnership agreement (who does what, how decisions are made, exit terms)

### Brand & Domain
- [ ] Confirm final business name (is "Momentum" available/right?)
- [ ] Check domain availability:
  - [ ] momentum.com.au
  - [ ] momentumteams.com.au
  - [ ] getmomentum.com.au
  - [ ] wearemomentum.com.au
- [ ] Purchase domain (Namecheap, VentraIP, Crazy Domains)
- [ ] Secure social handles (@momentum or variation on Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook)
- [ ] Create simple logo (can be text-based initially)

### Pricing
- [ ] Research competitor pricing (team-building facilitators, corporate wellness)
- [ ] Calculate costs (facilitator time, travel, materials, venue if needed)
- [ ] Set pricing for:
  - [ ] Half-day program
  - [ ] Full-day program
  - [ ] Per-person rate for larger groups
  - [ ] Travel fees (outside Brisbane metro)
  - [ ] Add-ons (ZenThai sessions, follow-up coaching)
- [ ] Decide on deposit/payment terms

## Phase 4: Sales & Marketing

### Warm Outreach (Start Here)
- [ ] List every contact who might:
  - Know someone in HR/L&D
  - Run a business with teams
  - Work at a school
  - Be involved in sports clubs
  - Organise events/conferences
- [ ] Personal outreach to warm contacts (email, LinkedIn, text)
- [ ] Ask for introductions, not sales
- [ ] Offer a discounted "pilot" session to first 2-3 clients in exchange for testimonials

### LinkedIn Strategy
- [ ] Optimise personal LinkedIn profiles (all three)
- [ ] Create LinkedIn company page
- [ ] Post 1-2x per week:
  - Behind-the-scenes prep
  - Research snippets (the science)
  - Short insights about team dynamics
  - Client wins (once you have them)
- [ ] Connect with HR managers, L&D professionals, People & Culture leads

### Other Channels (Later)
- [ ] Google Business Profile (for local search)
- [ ] Instagram (more relevant for schools, sports, events market)
- [ ] Attend HR/L&D networking events
- [ ] Reach out to event agencies and conference organisers
- [ ] Consider podcast guesting (Darren's profile helps here)

### Collateral
- [ ] One-page PDF for email attachments
- [ ] Short intro video (60-90 seconds, can be iPhone quality)
- [ ] Slide deck for formal pitches (if needed)

## Notes

- Don't wait for perfection. Launch with "good enough" and iterate.
- Real photos and testimonials matter more than fancy design.
- The first 3 clients will come from personal networks, not marketing.
- Every session is a chance to refine the offering and collect feedback.
- You can raise prices later. Start where you're confident.
