The Team

Three Specialists. One Vision.

Darren Percival

Voice & Expression

30+ years helping people find their voice. You might know him from The Voice Australia. We know him as someone who can get anyone singing—even the skeptics.

Darren is more than just a singer. He's one of Australia's most accomplished vocalists, a Gold record selling recording artist, gifted musician, dedicated songwriter, and renowned vocal coach.

He established The Singing Space in 1997 to offer individuals, gatherings and choirs the tools to unlock the barriers that stop them from singing and vocalising freely. His company Harmonious Collaborations facilitates offsite retreats for corporations.

The Voice Australia Gold Record Artist Vocal Coach The Singing Space

Linda Lou Küster

Movement & Bodywork

Psychology meets physical practice. Linda has this gift for creating space where people feel safe to let go. Yoga, QiGong, ZenThai—all in service of helping you feel at home in your body.

With an open mind and heart, Linda combines profound listening, body sensitivity and enormous joy in her offerings. She is passionate about facilitating transformational journeys for individuals and groups.

Connecting insights from her Master's in Psychology with her natural enthusiasm for movement, she is intrigued by the body-mind connection. She combines her wealth of knowledge with tapping into the wisdom of nature.

Psychology (Masters) ZenThai Shiatsu Yoga & QiGong Body-Mind Counselling

Dave Spicer

Music & Harmony

Jazz pianist, educator, quiet force. Dave's been teaching at Griffith Conservatorium for 20 years. His music creates the space where everything else becomes possible.

An award-winning jazz piano performer and educator since 1999, Dave brings expertise in composition, harmony and arranging. Regular performer at jazz festivals and international concert venues throughout Australia, USA, Europe, UK and NZ.

As an Official Australia/NZ Sponsored Artist for KAWAI pianos and finalist for the National Jazz Awards, Dave brings deep musical knowledge combined with a passion for vocal pedagogy.

KAWAI Artist Jazz Awards Finalist University Lecturer Griffith Conservatorium
