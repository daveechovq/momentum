Brand Essence

Warmth. Credibility. Transformation.

Momentum is a premium team experience offering that creates genuine connection through voice, movement, and music. The brand conveys sophistication that's approachable, confidence that's not corporate.

Logo Guidelines

  • Always use lowercase italic styling
  • Minimum clear space: Height of the "m" on all sides
  • Never stretch, distort, or change the font weight
  • Use Warm White (#FDFCFA) on dark backgrounds
  • Use Charcoal (#2C2C2C) on light backgrounds

Tone of Voice

How we speak

Warm but confident. Direct and honest. Conversational, never corporate. Our words should feel like they come from a real person who genuinely cares.

Do

  • Speak directly to the reader
  • Acknowledge real problems honestly
  • Use contractions (we'll, you're, isn't)
  • Let personality come through
  • Be confident without boasting

Don't

  • Use buzzwords (synergy, leverage, optimize)
  • Over-promise or use superlatives
  • Sound like a press release
  • Be preachy or condescending
  • Hide behind corporate language

Example Phrases

"Most team-building is forgettable. This isn't."

"Real connection isn't talked about — it's experienced."

"Three Specialists. One Vision."

"Let's have a conversation."
